Core Research Domains

1. Neurotransmitter Systems

Mechanistic mapping of dopaminergic, serotonergic, glutamatergic, and GABAergic pathways, with emphasis on receptor-level modulation and downstream behavioral effects.

2. Pharmacodynamics of CNS Agents

Studying ligand-receptor interactions, dose-response curves, and therapeutic index modeling for psychotropic compounds.

3. Behavioral Pharmacology

Investigating how pharmacological interventions shape cognition, mood, motivation, and executive function.

4. Translational Mechanisms

Converting complex neurochemical data into practical therapeutic frameworks.
