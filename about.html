Biography

Khalid Almansoori (born in the United Arab Emirates, age: your age) is an Emirati neuropsychopharmacology researcher. He is recognized as the first Emirati neuropsychopharmacologist, contributing to the emerging field of developmental and behavioral psychopharmacology in the UAE.
